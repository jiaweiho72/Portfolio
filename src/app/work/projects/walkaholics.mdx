## Overview

[View GitHub](https://github.com/Walkaholics/team_5378)

Developed a mobile application designed to empower users in managing their health and fitness. The app integrates geospatial mapping, drone imagery, and computer vision to monitor critical areas in Singapore, focusing on detecting obstructions in fire access routes and enhancing operational efficiency for safety inspections.

## Technologies Used
- Frontend: React Native
- Backend: Supabase
- Development Tools: Expo CLI, HTML, CSS, JavaScript


## Key Features

- **Health Report**: Visualizes and analyzes user health data, providing insights for better health management.
- **Health Metrics calculator**: Calculates Total Daily Energy Expenditure (TDEE) to help users understand their caloric needs.
- **Fitness and Diet Planner**: Allows users to plan, track, and edit their daily fitness routines and diets, with the ability to mark exercises as completed.

## Challenges Faced

Faced challenges in ensuring data accuracy and real-time synchronization across multiple user profiles. Implemented efficient state management and backend optimizations to address these issues.

## Outcome

The project not only enhanced my skills in mobile app development and health analytics but also demonstrated the potential for technology to facilitate healthier lifestyles. It showcases my ability to create user-friendly, data-driven applications that cater to individual health and fitness needs.
